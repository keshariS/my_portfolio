About Me

Hi, I'm Shuvam Keshari, a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of Texas at Austin being advised by Dr. Dhiraj Murthy and Dr. Anna Wilkinson. I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a focus on Power Electronics and Computer Vision.
Prior to joining UT, I have been implementing my expertise in these fields in developing really cool cars as an employee of Jaguar Land Rover since 2020. I aspire to use my skillset to develop products that realize my vision of a greener tomorrow through sustainable transportation where autonomous and connected cars are a common sight.
